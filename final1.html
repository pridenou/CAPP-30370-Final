Primer on Halo

It is the 26th century. Humanity finds itself caught in a war with an unknown alien force known as the Covenant. Human worlds are falling one by one to the Covenant armada in a war that has lasted nearly 10 years. The superior technology and size of the Covenant is proving to be too much for UNSC forces.

Humanity's last hope rests with the Master Chief, the last of the Spartans. A super soldier, genetically enhanced with unmatched combat skills, must find a way to halt the Covenant force from finding Earth--the last human stronghold.
